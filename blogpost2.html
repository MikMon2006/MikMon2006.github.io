Makeup Trends I hated and ABSOLUTELY LOVED in 2023.

In honour of the new year here is a list of trend I loved and hated in the year of 2023. Now that it’s 2024 I hope we can look forward to some amazing makeup trend and leave the ones I disliked behind.

Disclaimer: Doesn’t mean I don’t like these shouldn’t mean that you should stop doing them if you like them then you should keep liking them.

Let’s start off with the one I personally hated:

  1. Soap Brows: These kind of brows are a hit or a miss with me. Sometimes it suits some people and others it just downright looks bad.
  2. Clean Girl Makeup: Although to some extent I liked this I quickly grew bored of this makeup style and honestly it’s lacking that creativity I love in makeup so it lowkey sucks.
  3. Pink Under eyes: I just don’t think these would look good in real life besides camera. So yeah nice on camera a little whack in person.
  4. Overlining Lips: The same goes for overlined lips, nice on camera not so nice in person.
