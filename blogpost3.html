Blog Post 3

Here are my Favourite Purchases of 2023!

  1. Clinique Black Honey: Suits every skin tone looks absolutely gorgeous on the lips. They make my ashy lips look nice and gorgeous.
  2. Lottie London Diamond Shine: Absolutely Glittery and gorgeous on the cheeks and eyes my favourite shade is rose gold.
  3. NYX Butter Glosses: These come in every colour and are so comfortable on the lips.
  4. Flormar Lip Glaze: Super Glossy and gorgeous people sleep on these way too much.
  5. Essence Translucent Powder: Keeps makeup in place during those summer months and for a cheap price.
  6. Essence Lash princess: For a drugstore lash product it holds up through the day.
  7. Body Shop Sheer tint: Gorgeous colours and look amazing on everyone.
  8. Relove Lip oil: A good Lip oil for its cheap price and the flavours I recommend popping by lucy and getting yourself one.
  9. Technic Ibiza Palette: Buttery and Pigmented shades. You'll have every colour you'd ever need for various colourful eyelooks. If you're not a fan of bright bold colours though I recommend looking at technics other palettes as they are great quality.
  10. Revolution Earth Sign Palette: Got this as a gift and I love it. Perfect for zodiac fanatics like me and the colour story screams earth sign. Perfect for the autumn/spring months.
